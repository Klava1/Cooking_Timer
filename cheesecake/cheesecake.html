The Most Delicious Cottage Cheese Casserole

A classic comfort food, the Cottage Cheese Casserole is a beloved dish known for its delightful blend of flavors and textures. This casserole is a staple in many households due to its simple yet satisfying nature. Made with rich cottage cheese, it offers a creamy base that is both nutritious and delicious. The addition of ingredients like semolina and eggs creates a fluffy and light texture, while vanilla essence adds a subtle sweetness. Dried cranberries and raisins introduce a burst of fruity flavors, making each bite a delightful experience. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even as a dessert, this casserole can be enjoyed warm, topped with sour cream or jam, adding to its versatility and appeal.

Ingredients

  • Cottage Cheese - 500 g
  • Chicken Egg - 3 pieces
  • Sugar - 3 tablespoons
  • Semolina - 5 tablespoons
  • Vanilla Essence - 1 teaspoon
  • Baking Powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Dried Cranberries - to taste
  • Raisins - to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients except egg whites.
  2. Whip the egg whites in a separate bowl with a pinch of salt until soft peaks form. Gently fold into the cottage cheese mixture.
  3. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius. Pour the prepared mixture into a greased ovenproof dish and bake for about 45 minutes until the top turns golden brown.
  4. Serve the casserole warm, with sour cream or jam.
